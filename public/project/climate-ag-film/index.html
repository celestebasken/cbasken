Climate Change's Impact on Agriculture

Award-winning documentary.

Project Details

  • Role : producer, editor, interviewer
  • Competitions : 3 awards
  • Editing software : Final Cut Pro X
  • Date : Spring 2020
  • Grade : 10
  • Other contributors : C Rayburn, S Corrales

Award-winning climate and agriculture film

I co-created this documentary in spring of 2020 for the C-Span StudentCam documentary competition. My group shot original b-roll, conducted investigative research and interviewed farmers, policy experts and a sitting US Congressman.

The film was nominated for Fan Favorite and won Third Place in C-Span’s StudentCam documentary competition. It also won an award in the Ghandi Brigade Youth Media competition and was screened at the All-American High School Film Festival.
